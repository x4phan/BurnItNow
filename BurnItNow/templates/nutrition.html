Sausages

Meatballs

Meatballs are small balls of ground meat mixed with breadcrumbs, eggs, and seasonings. They can be made from beef, pork, chicken, or veal and are often served with sauce or as part of pasta dishes.

Duck Breast

Duck breast is the meat from the breast of a duck. It has a rich, flavorful taste and can be roasted, grilled, or pan-seared.

Venison

Venison is the meat of a deer. It is lean and rich in flavor, often used in dishes like stews, roasts, and steaks.

Ribs

Ribs are cuts of meat from the ribcage of animals, commonly pork or beef. They are often grilled, smoked, or roasted and served with barbecue sauce.

Liver

Liver is the organ meat of animals, often from cows or chickens. It is nutrient-dense and rich in vitamins and minerals. It can be pan-fried, grilled, or used in pâtés.

Salami

Salami is a type of cured sausage made from fermented and air-dried meat, usually pork. It is often sliced and eaten as a snack or added to sandwiches and pizzas.

Prosciutto

Prosciutto is a type of dry-cured ham that is usually thinly sliced and served uncooked. It has a delicate flavor and is often used in charcuterie boards or as a topping for pizzas and salads.
