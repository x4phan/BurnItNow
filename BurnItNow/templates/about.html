About Us

Our Journey Begins

It all started with a group of six college students, each with a passion for technology and a desire to make a positive impact. Among them was Dave Dominic Goze, our lead developer, whose love for coding drove the project forward.

A Common Goal

As part of our final project for our Intelligent Systems course, we set out to tackle a common problem: accurately tracking calorie expenditure during physical activities. It was a challenge we had all faced, and we were determined to find a solution.

The Birth of an Idea

Driven by our shared goal of helping others lead healthier lives, we came up with the concept for Calories Burnt Predictor. We wanted to create an app that could predict calorie burn with precision, making it easier for people to reach their fitness goals.

Dedication and Hard Work

With our vision in mind, we dove headfirst into the project. Late nights and early mornings became the norm as we coded, tested, and refined the app. Despite the challenges we faced along the way, our dedication never wavered.

A Dream Realized

After weeks of hard work, the app was finally ready. With its intuitive interface and accurate predictions, it exceeded our expectations. We couldn't wait to share it with the world and make a difference in people's lives.

About BurnItNow

Harnessing data from over 15,000 meticulously curated samples, our advanced machine learning model precisely forecasts the number of calories you burn during your workouts. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness journey, our platform is designed to empower you every step of the way.

At the heart of our app lies a commitment to accuracy and customization. By considering key factors such as age, gender, height, weight, exercise duration, heart rate, and body temperature, our algorithm tailors its predictions to your unique physiology and activity level. This level of personalized precision ensures that you receive actionable insights that are relevant to your specific fitness goals.

But our app is more than just a calorie counter. It's a comprehensive fitness companion, dedicated to providing you with the tools and knowledge you need to optimize your workouts and achieve your desired results. Whether you're looking to shed pounds, build muscle, or improve your overall health, our platform offers a wealth of resources.
